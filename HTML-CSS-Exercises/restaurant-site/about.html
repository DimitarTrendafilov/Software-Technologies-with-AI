Our Story

Harbor Street Kitchen began as a small twelve-seat cafe in 2005. We set out to serve honest coastal cooking with the freshest ingredients we could source from our neighbors and fishermen.

Today we are still family-owned, still greeting guests by name, and still crafting dishes that let the ingredients shine.

What We Care About

  • Partnering with local farms and fisheries.
  • Serving seasonal menus that change with the tides.
  • Creating a welcoming dining room for friends and families.
